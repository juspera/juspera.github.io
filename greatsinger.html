Faerinn Greatsinger for Mayor


From Juspera's player: A lawyer by trade, Faerinn Greatsinger is a deeply compassionate Half-Elf who has spent a good portion of his life assisting the downtrodden in claiming their rights. I have known Faerinn as well as his player for some time now and can wholeheartedly endorse the character for mayor, and can certify the player as an intelligent, thoughtful and extremely funny soul with whom I would be delighted to roleplay anytime.

A message from Mr. Greatsinger

Wehnimer’s Landing is the promise of independence. A place where anyone from any walk of life can just exist without the permission of someone “higher born” to do so. Despite these differences, we are a community that has always been able to come together and raise each other up under constant threat of eldritch annihilation.

Defending that freedom has come with its own scars. Where before we were free to be ourselves we must now kneel and beg permission like children asking to go out to play. Earl Jovery says yes for now, but here’s the thing about rule by birth. What would his children think of us and our freedoms? Or his children’s children? Or their children?

As mayor I would see to it the the noose slipped over our necks does not get any tighter. But I don’t want to see the Landing just survive. We’ve been just surviving for too long. Thanks to Mayor Lylia and our town’s defenders Wehnimer’s Landing can rebuild. This time we could thrive.



Learn more about Faerinn at his wiki page, or check out his campaign event logs. I hope to see everyone at the polls! - Juspera
