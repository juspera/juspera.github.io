Juspera's Good Ole GS4 Page

GS3 Page - Guides - History - Humor - Roleplaying - The Turnip


The Turnip was created in 2002 as a satire of GS3, Elanthia, Simutronics, the player base, GMs, and anything else that deserved to be made fun of. An equal-opportunity offender, the Turnip was unashamedly based on the Onion, one of our greatest national treasures. And now it's back. Writing The Turnip is more difficult than it used to be, thanks to the decline of OOCness, snertism and general bad behavior -- and also because I just don't have as much time as I used to. However, I'll throw another new issue up here whenever I get enough material.
